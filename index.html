Frequently Asked Questions

Our Experts Answers

Your Ultimate Resource: Unveiling Answers to Your Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for a Perfect Stay!

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in time is at 12:00 PM, and check-out time is at 10:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

Do you offer airport pick-up and drop-off services?

Yes, we provide airport transfer services for our guests. Please contact our concierge desk in advance to arrange your transportation.

Do you provide trekking or sightseeing tours?

Yes, we can arrange trekking, sightseeing, and adventure tours for our guests through our trusted local partners. Please inquire at the front desk for more information and booking assistance.

Is Wi-Fi available in the rooms?

Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available in all rooms and public areas of the hotel, allowing you to stay connected throughout your stay.

Are there dining options available at the hotel?

Yes, we have an on-site restaurant serving a variety of delicious dishes, including local Ladakhi cuisine and international favorites. Guests can also enjoy outdoor dining with breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.

Is there parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we offer complimentary parking facilities for our guests. Our secure parking area ensures the safety of your vehicle during your stay.
