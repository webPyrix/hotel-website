Tranquil Haven

Shanti Stupa

Shanti Stupa is a symbol of peace and harmony. Built by Japanese Buddhist monks to promote world peace, this gleaming white stupa offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. Visitors can climb the steps to reach the stupa and marvel at its intricate carvings, colorful murals, and serene atmosphere.

Azure Serenity

Pangong Lake

Pangong Lake, nestled amidst the Himalayas, captivates with its crystal-clear waters mirroring the sky's vivid hues. This transboundary marvel, shared by India and China, invites nature lovers and photographers. Enjoy serene walks, adventurous camping, and unforgettable vistas. Experience Pangong Lake's ethereal charm firsthand.

Tranquil Oasis

Nubra Valley

Nubra Valley, nestled amidst the Himalayas, is a serene oasis offering surreal landscapes and lush greenery. With its unique high-altitude desert terrain, visitors can enjoy camel safaris, explore ancient monasteries, or simply bask in its peaceful ambiance. Escape the ordinary and discover moments of wonder in Nubra Valley.

Spiritual Sanctuary

Diskit Gompa

Diskit Monastery, a historic gem in Ladakh, stands proudly atop a rugged hill. Founded in the 14th century, it offers a window into the region's cultural legacy. Its whitewashed walls and golden spires radiate serenity. Explore its corridors adorned with vibrant murals, engage in rituals, and soak in the tranquility. With vistas of majestic mountains, Diskit Monastery promises an unforgettable spiritual journey.

Epic Expedition

Khardung La Pass

Khardung La Pass, standing over 5,300 meters high, is a symbol of human determination. As one of the world's highest motorable passes, it's a thrilling challenge for adventurers. The thin air, lunar-like landscape, and surreal views make the ascent unforgettable. Reaching the summit offers a sense of achievement and breathtaking panoramas of the Ladakh Range. Every journey through Khardung La Pass, whether by bike or car, is an epic adventure to cherish forever.
